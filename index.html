### Biography Hengcan Shi is a Research Fellow at *Data Science & AI Department*, *Monash University*. Before that, he was a Senior Researcher at *Tencent*. He received his Ph.D. degree in computer vision from *University of Electronic Science and Technology of China* in 2020. His research interests include image segmentation, object detection, machine learning and Vision+Language. He has served as reviewer for *IEEE T-PAMI*, *IJCV*, *T-MM*, *T-CSVT*, *TIE* and *Neurocomputing*.
