# üìñ About Me
Hengcan Shi (Áü≥ÊÅíÁí®) is a Professor at *the School of Artificial Intelligence and Robotics*, *Hunan University*. Before that, he was an Associate Professor at *the College of Electrical and Information Engineering*, *Hunan University*, a Research Fellow at *the Department of Data Science & AI*, *Monash University*, and a Senior Researcher at *Tencent*. He received his Ph.D. degree in computer vision from *University of Electronic Science and Technology of China* in 2019. He has served as reviewer for *IEEE TPAMI*, *IJCV*, *IEEE TIP*, *TMM*, *TCSVT* and *TIE*. He was the Web Chair, Area Chair and PC member of *ACMMM*, *CVPR*, *ICCV*, *ECCV*, *AAAI*, *IJCAI*, *ICML* *ICLR* and *NeurIPS*.

His research interests include Image Segmentation, Object Detection, Vision+Language and Large Model.
